Ayurveda- The way of living

Vatta Dosha

In Ayurveda, Vata represents the air and ether elements. It governs bodily functions like movement, communication, and creativity. Imbalance can lead to anxiety, dryness, and digestive issues.

Pitta Dosha

Pitta embodies the fire and water elements, controlling metabolism, digestion, and body temperature. An excess can result in irritability, inflammation, and skin problems.

Kapha Dosha

Kapha is composed of earth and water, governing stability, structure, and bodily lubrication. When imbalanced, it can cause lethargy, weight gain, and congestion.
